Features Implemented:

The menu project opens with a welcoming â€œWelcome!â€ message for new users and then gives them a choice between 5 different food categories: Korean, Japanese, Italian, Mexican, and American. Users will select their preference of the type of food and receive a menu based on their selection. Then they will select a food from their menu and get the message "Please enjoy your meal!", but in a different language (depending on preference) and an emoji unique to the chosen food. If the user inputs an item that does not match with any of the options available, they will be asked if they would like to try again.
